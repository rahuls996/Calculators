What is the Ideal Health Insurance Coverage?

The ideal health insurance coverage for an Indian family with aging parents, spouse, and children can be twice the family's annual income. However, you can choose to get higher or lower coverage as per your needs. Opting for a ₹10–₹25 lakh plan can prove to be sufficient for one year.

Here are the other factors you can consider to calculate your ideal coverage:

  • Location: Consider your city of residence — healthcare costs are higher in tier 1 cities.
  • Age: The age of the oldest member and their medical needs can help you decide your ideal cover.
  • Income: Your income affects the premium you can comfortably pay and the coverage you need.

Factors affecting your premium

Several factors influence the premium you pay for health insurance. Understanding them can help you choose the right plan.

  • Age: Older individuals typically pay higher premiums due to increased health risks.
  • City: Metro cities with higher healthcare costs attract higher premiums.
  • Cover amount: Higher sum insured means higher premium.
  • Number of members: Family floater plans cost more but offer better value.
